Our current use of cookies

This website does not use analytics, advertising trackers, social-media embeds or cookie-based personalisation. The embedded MailerLite signup form may use limited cookies or browser storage to operate the form and remember that it has been submitted. These technologies support the newsletter signup requested by the visitor; they are not used by us for advertising.

External links

The site links to services such as Instagram and email. Those services are separate websites and may use their own cookies after you follow a link. Their own privacy and cookie information applies once you leave this website.

Future changes

If we introduce analytics, embedded media or another feature that uses non-essential cookies or similar technologies, we will update this policy and ask for consent where required.
